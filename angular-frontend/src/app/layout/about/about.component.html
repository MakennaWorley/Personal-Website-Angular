2016: Software Modding

My journey in Computer Science officially started around 2016, when I modded my New 3DS XL that I had received for Christmas in 2015. The main goal was modifying my Pokémon save. To this day, I continue to mod my personal Nintendo and PlayStation consoles.

So far, I have soft-modded: 2x Wiis, 1x Wii U, 3x 3DS/2DS systems, 2x Switches, 1x PSP, and 1x PS Vita. Although I mostly focus on the software side of modding, I have also modded the occasional Game Boy with new screens/shells and replaced batteries in old Pokémon games from the Game Boy era.
